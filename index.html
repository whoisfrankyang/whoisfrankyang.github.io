Hi! I'm Bohan Yang, a senior student at Cornell University pursuing a B.S. with Honors in Computer Science.

I am an undergrad researcher at the Cornell EmPRISE Lab. I am grateful to have worked with Prof. Tapomayukh Bhattacharjee and Prof. Sarah Dean on assistive robotics. I'm also a part-time Machine Learning Engineer at Data-to-Decision working with Prof. Weiqing Gu on medical speech/voice machine learning models.

Research

My interests lie at the intersection of robotics and machine learning, specifically focusing on post-deployment robot learning — how robots can recognize uncertainty, ask for help when needed, and improve and learn continuously over their lifetime in real-world environments.

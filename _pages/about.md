My current research focuses on leveraging Language Models to tackle mathematical problems, particularly in the realm of symbolic regression. Additionally, I am exploring the application of deep learning techniques for 3D CAD reconstruction.

I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence and its applications, particularly its application in biomedical and manufacturing industry. I am always eager to collaborate and learn more in these exciting fields.
